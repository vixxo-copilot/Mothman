# Vixxo COI — Endorsement Equivalents Reference

Authoritative companion to `vixxo-requirements.md`. Maps every provision in the **Sample COI 2026** Description of Operations to the ISO endorsements named on the certificate and commonly accepted equivalent forms.

Source: Vixxo Sample COI 2026 (Description of Operations block).

---

## Description of Operations — provisions to satisfy

The Sample COI Description of Operations requires all of the following:

1. **Additional Insured — General Liability** (ongoing and completed operations), per written agreement
2. **Additional Insured — Automobile Liability**, per written agreement
3. **Primary insurance** — GL is primary to any other available insurance
4. **Waiver of Subrogation** — GL, Auto, and WC/Employers Liability
5. **30 days' Notice of Cancellation**

The sample explicitly names three ISO forms; the other provisions are contractual language that should be backed by policy endorsements where possible.

## 1. Additional Insured — General Liability (Ongoing Operations)

**Named on Sample COI:** `CG 20 10` Edition `04/13` (or equivalent)

| Form | Edition(s) | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **CG 20 10** | **04/13** (preferred) | Additional Insured — Owners, Lessees or Contractors — Scheduled Person or Organization (ongoing ops) |
| CG 20 10 | 07/04, 10/01, 11/85 | Older scheduled ongoing-ops editions; commonly accepted as equivalent |
| **CG 20 33** | 04/13 and prior | Blanket/automatic AI when required in a written contract (ongoing + completed) |
| **CG 20 38** | 04/13 and prior | Automatic AI for ongoing operations when required in agreement |
| CG 20 26 | Various | Designated Person or Organization — **not a full substitute** for CG 20 10 + CG 20 37 in vendor/subcontractor scenarios |
| CG 20 42 / CG 20 43 | 04/13 era | Designated-operations variants (niche use) |

**Vixxo rule:** CG 20 10 alone is **not** sufficient. Completed operations coverage (CG 20 37 or equivalent) is also required.

## 3. Additional Insured — Automobile Liability

**Named on Sample COI:** `CA 20 48` Edition `10/13` (or equivalent)

| Form | Edition(s) | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **CA 20 48** | **10/13** (preferred) | Designated Insured / Additional Insured for Business Auto when required by written contract |
| CA 20 48 | 10/01 and prior | Older editions; commonly accepted |
| **Carrier proprietary equivalents** | — | Acceptable if functionally equivalent; Vixxo has accepted: **State Farm CMP-4786**, **Auto-Owners 58503** (Designated Insured) |

Non-ISO carriers often use proprietary forms instead of CA 20 48. Accept when they grant designated/additional insured status on auto liability per written contract.

## 4. Primary (and Non-Contributory) — General Liability

**Sample COI language:** *"The insurance provided by the General Liability policy shall be primary to any other insurance coverage available."*

| Form | Edition(s) | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **CG 20 01** | **04/13** (preferred) | Primary and Noncontributory — Other Insurance Condition |
| CG 20 01 | 07/04, 01/96 | Older primary/non-contributory editions |
| Manuscript / policy language | — | Custom endorsement with equivalent primary-and-non-contributory language |

**Note:** Business Auto is typically primary by policy form without a separate endorsement. Vixxo still requires the **primary language on the COI** for GL.

## 8. 30 Days' Notice of Cancellation

**Sample COI language:** *"30 days' Notice of Cancellation applies."*

| Form | Edition(s) | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **IL 01 03** | 11/85+ | Notice of Cancellation to Designated Additional Insured or Mortgagee (interline — applies across policy types) |
| **CG 02 24** | 10/93+ | Earlier Notice of Cancellation Provided By Us (CGL; schedule specifies number of days, e.g. 30) |
| State-specific NOC endorsements | Varies | Many carriers use state-modified cancellation notice forms |
| Manuscript NOC endorsement | — | Custom 30-day notice to certificate holder / designated party |

**Important:** Language in the COI Description of Operations alone does **not** create enforceable notice rights. The underlying policy should carry a notice-of-cancellation endorsement naming Vixxo (or the certificate holder). Vixxo accepts the COI language as a compliance checkpoint; best practice is to also collect the actual NOC endorsement.

## Practical acceptance notes (Vixxo COI review)

- **All three named AI forms are mandatory:** CG 20 10 **and** CG 20 37 **and** CA 20 48 (or accepted equivalents). CG 20 10 without CG 20 37 is a common deficiency.
- **"Or equivalent"** means same coverage scope, not just the words "additional insured" on the certificate.
- **Waiver of subrogation** must be confirmed on **each line separately** (GL, Auto, WC/EL). A generic "WOS applies" without specifying lines → flag as **Needs clarification**.
- **Carrier proprietary forms** are acceptable when they match the ISO form's intent. Examples already seen in Vixxo intake: State Farm **CMP-4786** (≈ CA 20 48), Auto-Owners **58503** (Designated Insured).
- The full Vixxo Additional Insured party list (Vixxo, subsidiaries, customers, site owners/operators/mortgagees as "Insurance Beneficiaries") must appear in the Description of Operations or on the endorsements. A bare "Vixxo Corporation is Additional Insured" is **not** sufficient.
