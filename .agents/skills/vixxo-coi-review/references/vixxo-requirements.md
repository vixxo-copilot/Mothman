# Vixxo Minimum Insurance Requirements — Reference

This is the authoritative requirement list. Read it at the start of every COI review. The verbatim wording below comes from Vixxo's sample/standard COI language and must be matched on the service provider's certificate.

---

## 1. Required coverage lines and limits

A complete, acceptable COI must include all of the following coverages with limit boxes populated. When the user has not specified Vixxo's contracted dollar minimums for a given SP, treat any blank or "$0" limit field as a deficiency, and any unusually low number as "needs clarification."

### Commercial General Liability (occurrence form)
- Each Occurrence
- Damage to Rented Premises (each occurrence)
- Med Exp (any one person)
- Personal & Adv Injury
- General Aggregate
- Products – Comp/Op Aggregate
- Aggregate limit applies per: Policy / Project / Loc — must be checked

### Automobile Liability
- Combined Single Limit (each accident) **or** Bodily Injury (per person) + Bodily Injury (per accident) + Property Damage (per accident)
- One of the following auto-coverage scope boxes must be checked: Any Auto / All Owned Autos / Scheduled Autos / Hired Autos / Non-Owned Autos. "Any Auto" or the combination of Owned + Hired + Non-Owned is preferred.

### Umbrella / Excess Liability
- Required when underlying GL or Auto limits do not meet the contracted total. Occurrence form preferred. Each Occurrence and Aggregate must be populated.

### Workers' Compensation and Employers' Liability
- WC Statutory Limits — must be indicated
- E.L. Each Accident
- E.L. Disease – Each Employee
- E.L. Disease – Policy Limit
- "Any Proprietor/Partner/Executive Officer/Member Excluded?" — if Yes, an explanation must appear under Description of Operations.

## 2. Additional Insured wording (verbatim — must appear on the COI)

The following exact language must be present in the Description of Operations / Locations / Vehicles section, or in the attached endorsements:

> Vixxo Corporation, its subsidiaries, affiliates, related entities and their officers, officials, employees, volunteers (collectively, "Vixxo"), its customers, the owner, operator and, if required, mortgagee of any site where SP performs Services (each an "Insurance Beneficiary" and collectively, "Insurance Beneficiaries") are included as Additional Insured with respect to the General Liability as per Endorsement CG 20 10 Edition 04/13 (or equivalent), Endorsement CG 20 37, Edition 04/13 (or equivalent) and the Automobile Liability policy as per Endorsement CA 20 48, Edition 10/13 (or equivalent) as required by written agreement. The insurance provided by the General Liability policy shall be primary to any other insurance coverage available. Waiver of Subrogation applies to Additional Insured with respect to the General Liability, Automobile Liability and Workers Compensation/Employers Liability policies as required by written agreement. 30 days' Notice of Cancellation applies.

A generic "Vixxo Corporation is listed as Additional Insured" is **not** sufficient. The full block above (or wording that captures every element of it) is required.

---

## 3. Required endorsements (form numbers and editions)

| Coverage | Endorsement | Edition | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| General Liability | CG 20 10 | 04/13 (or equivalent) | Additional Insured – Ongoing Operations |
| General Liability | CG 20 37 | 04/13 (or equivalent) | Additional Insured – Completed Operations |
| Automobile Liability | CA 20 48 | 10/13 (or equivalent) | Additional Insured – Auto |

All three are required. CG 20 10 alone does not satisfy the requirement — CG 20 37 must also be present for completed operations.

---

## 4. Other required provisions

- **Primary and non-contributory** — the General Liability policy must be primary to any other insurance available to Vixxo.
- **Waiver of Subrogation** — must apply on all three of: General Liability, Automobile Liability, and Workers' Compensation / Employers' Liability, in favor of the Additional Insureds.
- **Notice of Cancellation** — 30 days' notice required.

## 6. Per-requirement checklist (use this when reviewing)

For each item below, mark Compliant / Missing / Deficient / Needs clarification.

1. Insured name matches the SP's contracting legal name
2. Policy dates current (not expired; flag if expiring within 30 days)
3. CGL — occurrence form, all six limit fields populated
4. CGL — aggregate-applies-per box selected
5. Auto Liability — limit(s) populated and scope box checked
6. Umbrella/Excess — present if needed to reach required totals
7. WC — statutory box indicated
8. EL — Each Accident populated
9. EL — Disease Each Employee populated
10. EL — Disease Policy Limit populated
11. Proprietor/Partner exclusion question answered (and explained if Yes)
12. Additional Insured — full Vixxo wording present (verbatim or materially equivalent)
13. Endorsement CG 20 10 04/13 (or equivalent) — referenced on COI
14. Endorsement CG 20 37 04/13 (or equivalent) — referenced on COI
15. Endorsement CA 20 48 10/13 (or equivalent) — referenced on COI
16. Primary and non-contributory wording — present for GL
17. Waiver of Subrogation — GL
18. Waiver of Subrogation — Auto
19. Waiver of Subrogation — WC / EL
20. 30 days' Notice of Cancellation — referenced
21. Certificate Holder — exact name, street, suite, city, state, ZIP
